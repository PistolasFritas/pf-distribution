The Mezcal

Made by one family. Recognized by those who judge mezcal for a living.

Mr. Mezcal is a family-owned, single-source mezcal from Santiago Matatlán, Oaxaca. Three generations of the same family make it entirely by hand — no industrial machines, no blending plants, and no spirit bought on the open market. Every bottle traces back to one palenque and one family's agave.

A 42% ABV artesanal mezcal, made slowly and patiently, that has earned top honors at the competitions buyers and connoisseurs actually watch.

Compliance · Three-Tier System

Trade only. Licensed businesses only.

PF Distribution is a licensed alcohol wholesaler based in Williamsburg, Missouri, and the Missouri distribution partner for Mr. Mezcal. We sell only to properly licensed businesses authorized to purchase alcoholic beverages for resale — bars, restaurants, hotels, liquor stores, grocery stores, private clubs, event venues, caterers, and other licensed alcohol retailers. We do not sell directly to consumers, and we do not offer online checkout, consumer pricing, home delivery, or retail sales to the general public.

We operate within the three-tier alcohol distribution system — producer, distributor, retailer — and supply only the retailer tier. Product availability is subject to applicable federal, state, and local licensing requirements and may be limited to approved territories.
